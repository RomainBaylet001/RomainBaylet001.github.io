About Romain

Fine art photographer with a love for light, silence and real emotion.

I’m Romain, a photographer obsessed with atmosphere. Before stepping fully into photography, I spent years in fine dining and wine, learning how to read people, rooms and tiny details. Now I do the same thing with a camera.

My work is calm, intentional and focused on genuine connection. I’m not interested in forced smiles or stiff poses. I’d rather wait for that one subtle gesture that actually feels like you.

I split my time between Europe and Asia, available for weddings, portraits and personal projects worldwide.

If you’d like to work together or have a project in mind, I’d love to hear from you.
